About Namma Kudla

A student project focused on promoting local tourism and storytelling in Mangalore (Kudla).

Mission

To showcase Kudla’s cultural heritage, food and natural beauty through authentic travel stories and local guides.

Scope

Responsive front-end built with HTML, CSS and JavaScript. Features include posts, destination guides, and guide profiles.
